What is PCOD?
PCOD (Polycystic Ovary Disease) is a hormonal condition where the ovaries produce excess androgens. It causes irregular periods, acne, weight gain, and small cysts on ovaries. It affects about 1 in 5 women globally — and 70% go undiagnosed. Our app helps with early detection! 🌸
